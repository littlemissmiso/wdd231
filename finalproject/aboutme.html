Book Reviewer Extraordinaire

Hi there!


I’m Little Miss Miso, a 23-year-old book reviewer, miniature crafter, and cat enthusiast. I created this little corner of the internet to share the things I love most: stories, creativity, and cozy moments.

My love of reading started when I was a kid. Books were my safe place—offering adventure, comfort, and connection when I needed it most. Over time, reading became more than just a pastime. It turned into a passion that shaped how I see the world. I started this website to share honest book reviews and help others discover stories that speak to them.

When I’m not buried in a book, you’ll usually find me in the kitchen trying new recipes (miso soup is a favorite), crafting tiny dioramas, or heading outdoors for a hike. I also cherish time with my family—whether we’re cooking together, chatting about life, or enjoying quiet weekends, they’ve always been my biggest support.

And of course, my cats are always close by—usually snoozing beside me or batting at my craft supplies.

Thanks for stopping by—I hope you find something here that connects with you or inspires you for your next adventure!

Cheerio,
Little Miss Miso

Reading Fact 1

Just 6 minutes of reading can reduce stress by up to 68%, according to a study by the University of Sussex — faster than listening to music or going for a walk!

Reading Fact 2

A Yale study found that people who read books regularly live an average of 2 years longer than non-readers. Time to hit the library!

Reading Fact 3

Reading fiction — especially character-driven novels — actually boosts your brain’s ability to understand and connect with other people’s feelings.

Reading Fact 4

When you read about action, sights, or smells, your brain lights up as if you're experiencing them for real. Reading literally makes your brain simulate the story!
