SITE PLAN DETAILS

Site Name:

"The Literary Loft with LittleMissMiso"
The site will be a cozy and classy book review resource. ‘The Literary Loft’ fit that general tone, and ‘LittleMissMiso’ is my GitHub name, so it seemed fitting to have that be the hypothetical reviewer/owner of the page.

Site Purpose:

The site will be a book review resources geared towards both young adult and adult women. It will contain a Home page, a Book List page (with brief reviews), and an About Me page. It would help a hypothetical viewer to discover new book recommendations, and to build a connection to LittleMissMiso as a book reviewer, thus ensuring future returns to the site.

Site Scenarios:

1. What books would be great for [insert here] genre?

2. I trust LittleMissMiso’s opinion since she has similar interests. What books has she liked that I might like too?

3. I’m going on vacation. What would be a nice casual read?
